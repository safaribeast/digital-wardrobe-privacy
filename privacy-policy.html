Digital Wardrobe Privacy Policy

Last updated: September 14, 2025

Introduction

Digital Wardrobe ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Personal Information

  • Name: Collected for account creation and personalization
  • Email Address: Required for account authentication via Google OAuth

Photos and Media

  • Clothing Photos: Images you upload to build your digital wardrobe
  • These photos are analyzed using AI to identify clothing attributes (color, type, style)

Location Information

  • Approximate Location: Used temporarily to provide weather-based outfit recommendations
  • Location data is processed ephemerally and not stored permanently

Financial Information

  • Payment Information: Processed securely for premium features
  • Purchase History: Records of transactions for account management

App Activity

  • App Interactions: How you navigate and use the app
  • Search History: Your searches within the app for personalization
  • User-Generated Content: Your outfit preferences, notes, and saved combinations

How We Use Your Information

Core App Functionality

  • Authenticate your account and enable app features
  • Process and analyze your clothing photos using AI
  • Generate personalized outfit recommendations
  • Provide weather-based suggestions using your location
  • Process payments for premium features

Personalization

  • Customize outfit suggestions based on your wardrobe and preferences
  • Improve recommendations using your search and interaction history
  • Remember your style preferences and favorite combinations

Analytics and Improvement

  • Monitor app performance and identify bugs
  • Understand how users interact with different features
  • Improve the app experience based on usage patterns

Information Sharing

Third-Party Services

We share certain data with trusted third-party services to provide app functionality:

  • Claude (Anthropic): AI photo analysis for clothing recognition (photos only)
  • OpenWeatherMap: Weather data for outfit suggestions (location only)
  • PesaPal: Payment processing (payment info and purchase history only)

Data Security

  • All data is encrypted in transit using secure connections
  • We implement industry-standard security measures
  • Third-party services are selected based on their security standards

Your Rights and Choices

Data Collection Choices

  • Optional Data: Photos, location, search history, and user-generated content are collected only when you choose to provide them
  • Required Data: Name, email, app interactions, and payment info (when making purchases) are necessary for core functionality

Data Deletion

  • Delete Account: Request complete account deletion and removal of all associated data
  • Manage Data: Request deletion of specific data (activity history, photos, etc.) without deleting your entire account

Data Retention

We retain your information only as long as necessary to provide our services and comply with legal obligations. When you delete your account, we permanently remove your personal data from our systems.

Children's Privacy

Digital Wardrobe is not intended for use by children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

International Users

If you are accessing Digital Wardrobe from outside your country of residence, please be aware that your information may be transferred to, stored, and processed in countries where our servers are located.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy in the app and updating the "Last updated" date.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices, please contact us:

  • Account Deletion: Submit deletion request
  • Data Management: Manage your data
  • General Inquiries: Contact us through the app or your app store listing
